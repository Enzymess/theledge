Our Story

Where Mist Meets
Mountain Air

Perched on the hillsides of Baguio City, The Ledge is an intimate bed & breakfast celebrated for its panoramic views, clean and serene atmosphere, and warm Filipino hospitality. Whether you're watching the fog drift in at dawn or gazing at the city lights at dusk — every moment here feels like a breath of fresh air.

Free breakfast is included with every stay, and our curated in-house experiences — from paint challenges to board games — make The Ledge feel like a home away from home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

You can book directly through our website using the reservation form, or message us on our official Facebook page at facebook.com/TheLedgeBaguio. We also accept bookings via Cloudbeds. We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December–January and summer months).

Yes! A complimentary breakfast is included with every room booking. It's served fresh every morning and best enjoyed on the viewing deck with a cup of hot coffee and the city below.

Check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be arranged depending on availability — please message us in advance to request this.

The Ledge is a short drive or tricycle ride from the main city center. It's conveniently located near popular spots like Session Road, Burnham Park, and various Baguio dining establishments. Exact directions are sent upon confirmed booking.

Limited parking is available near the property. We recommend confirming parking arrangements when you message us to book, especially during holidays and weekends when the area gets busy.

Currently, The Ledge is not pet-friendly to ensure a comfortable and allergen-free environment for all guests. We appreciate your understanding.

Cancellations made at least 72 hours before your check-in date are eligible for a full refund or rebooking. Cancellations made within 72 hours may be subject to a partial fee. Please reach out to us directly for special circumstances.

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property including all rooms and common areas. Connection is reliable for both work and streaming.
